ABOUT US!

Hello, World! We are the AtomHacks Committee in Bronx Science, a team dedicated to helping others explore coding, whether they are beginners or advanced. Through workshops and opportunities to work in teams, we hope to create a friendly and enjoyable environment where anyone can inquire ideas based on the skillset that they are exposed to. Coding shouldn't be intimidating, and we hope that through this hackathon, we can bring our school community together, especially during these tough times.

Theme

This year, in lieu of these tough times, we want to use the power of coding to express activism. As we have been surrounded by an environment filled with challenges in 2020, we want to take advantage of the virtual environment of coding and empower others to do more, despite the challenges of virtual environments. Our team decided on the activism theme because of how broad and all-encompassing it is. Activism can include, but is not limited to, political activism, social activism, health activism and so much more.
